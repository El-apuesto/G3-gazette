## Annual Subscriptions

**Within 30 Days**
- Full refund if you're not satisfied

**After 30 Days**

## Non-Refundable Situations

- Account terminated for Terms of Service violations
- Third-party fees (payment processing, taxes)
- Services already rendered or grants already found
- Repeated refund requests (abuse prevention)
