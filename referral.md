# Referral Program

## Share Grant Geenie, Earn Rewards

<!-- TODO: Add referral program details and tracking -->

Help us spread the word about Grant Geenie and earn rewards for every person you refer!

### How It Works

1. **Sign up** for a free Grant Geenie account
2. **Get your unique referral link** from your dashboard
3. **Share** your link with friends, colleagues, and your network
4. **Earn rewards** when they sign up and subscribe

### Referral Rewards

- **$20 credit** for each friend who signs up for a paid plan
- **10% recurring commission** on their subscription (for Pro members)
- **Bonus rewards** at milestone referrals (5, 10, 25, 50+ referrals)

### Who Can You Refer?

- Nonprofit organizations
- Artists and creatives
- Small businesses and solopreneurs
- Researchers and academics
- Community organizations
- Anyone looking for grant funding!

### Referral Best Practices

**Personal Touch**
Share your own experience with Grant Geenie and how it's helped you.

**Social Sharing**
Post on LinkedIn, Twitter, Facebook, or relevant online communities.

**Email**
Send personalized emails to colleagues who might benefit.

**Content Creation**
Write blog posts or create videos about your grant-seeking journey.
