## Introduction

Grant Hustle ("we," "us," "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data.

## Information We Collect

**Account Information**
- Name and email address
- Organization name (if applicable)
- Password (encrypted)

**Usage Data**
- Search queries and filters
- Pages visited and features used
- Device and browser information
- IP address and location data

**Payment Information**
- Credit card information (processed by Stripe)
- Billing address
- Transaction history

## How We Use Your Information

- Provide and improve our services
- Send grant alerts and notifications
- Process payments and prevent fraud
- Communicate updates and news
- Analyze usage patterns and optimize performance
- Comply with legal obligations

## Information Sharing

We do NOT sell your personal information.

We may share data with:
- Service providers (hosting, payment processing, analytics)
- Legal authorities when required by law
- Business partners with your consent

## Your Rights

**Access** - Request copies of your personal data

**Correction** - Request correction of inaccurate data

**Deletion** - Request deletion of your data

**Opt-Out** - Unsubscribe from marketing emails

**Portability** - Request data in machine-readable format

## Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:
- Encryption in transit and at rest
- Regular security audits
- Access controls and authentication
- Secure data centers

## Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies for:
- Essential site functionality
- Analytics and performance monitoring
- Personalization and preferences

You can control cookies through browser settings.

## Third-Party Services

**We use:**
- Google Analytics (analytics)
- Stripe (payment processing)
- Vercel (hosting)
- Supabase (database)

These services have their own privacy policies.

## Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
